Frequently Asked Questions

Where will the Summer Ball be hosted?

The Summer Ball will be hosted at the Princess Royal Preforming Arts Center.

When Is the event?

The event will be hosted on June, Friday the 13th, 2025.

How can I purchase tickets?

Tickets can be purchased from, Delancy, Coutanchez or Preforming Art Center receptions for £10 each.

Can I bring my friends or partner to the Summer Ball?

We encourage you to bring your plus ones to the Summer Ball we only require them to be above the age of 16 and have photographic ID if they're over 18.

What will the dress code be? Do I have to be in a suit/dress?

No! This year we've gotten rid of the dress code as long as it is appropriate you will be admitted.
